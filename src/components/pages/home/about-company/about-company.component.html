About VT Platform

The VT platform is a Saudi platform for issuing maintenance contracts for security camera devices. Contracts are issued by entities approved and licensed by the Supreme Commission for Industrial Security. The platform is characterized by ease of operation and does not require communication with anyone to issue or renew contracts or maintain requests.
